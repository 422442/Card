# Business Card Generator

A professional business card generator with 30 customizable templates, live preview, and export functionality.

![Business Card Generator](https://img.shields.io/badge/version-1.0-blue) ![HTML](https://img.shields.io/badge/HTML5-E34F26?logo=html5&logoColor=white) ![CSS](https://img.shields.io/badge/CSS3-1572B6?logo=css3&logoColor=white) ![JavaScript](https://img.shields.io/badge/JavaScript-F7DF1E?logo=javascript&logoColor=black)

## Features

- **30 Professional Templates** - Modern, Minimal, Elegant, Creative, Corporate, and more
- **Live Preview** - See changes in real-time as you edit
- **Mobile Responsive** - Works perfectly on all devices with tab-based navigation
- **Export Options** - Download as PNG or JPG in high quality (1500x900px)
- **Easy Customization** - Edit name, job title, company, email, phone, and website

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Choose a template from the Templates tab
3. Fill in your details in the Edit tab
4. Preview your card and export as PNG or JPG
